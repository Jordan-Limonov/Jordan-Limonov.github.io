Jordan Limonov is the current head of the Association of Curators, whose mission it is to document as much of history, science, geography, and culture in the universe as possible. He is also the currently appointed Head Historian of the Stazian Republic. Mr. Limonov himself has written many works that summarized various times of interest, and is always eager to find more new time periods to explore and record. Mr. Limonov is currently writing a book named "Persistence," which details the exploits of the Cardina, a mercenary group closely associated with the Stazian Republic.
