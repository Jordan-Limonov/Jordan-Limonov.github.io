Jordan Limonov is the current head of the Association of Curators, whose mission is to document as much of history, science, geography, and culture in the universe as possible. They are also the currently appointed Head Historian of the Stazian Republic. Jordan has written many works that summarized various times of interest, and is always eager to find more new time periods to explore and record. Jordan is currently writing a book named "False Gods," which details the exploits of the Cardina, a mercenary group closely associated with the Stazian Republic. In their free time, Jordan likes to indulge in a variety of media from the Nàviavan Era. Their colleagues believe that their tastes are outdated, even in Nàviavan times.
